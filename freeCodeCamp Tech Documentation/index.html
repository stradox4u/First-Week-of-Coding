Rogaine Drug Facts

Active Ingredient Warning Directions Other Information Specifications

Active Ingredient

Minoxidil 5% w.v. - Hair regrowth treatment for men

Warning

For external use by men only.

Flammable: Keep away from fire or flame.

    Do not use if:

  • you are a woman
  • your amount of hair loss is different than that shown on the attached image, or your hair loss is n the front of the scalp. 5% minoxidil topical solution is not intended for frontal baldness or receding hairline.
  • you have no family history of hair loss
  • your hair loss is sudden and patchy
  • you do not know the reason for your hair loss
  • you are under 18 years of age. Do not use on babies and children.
  • your scalp is red, inflamed, infected, irritated or painful
  • you use other medicines on the scalp

Directions

  • apply one mL with dropper, 2 times a day directly onto the scalp in the hair loss area
  • using more volume, or more often will not improve results
  • continued use is necessary to increase and keep your hair regrowth, or hair loss will begin again

Other Information

  • see attached hair loss pictures
  • before use, read all included information
  • keep the box, it contains relevant information
  • hair regrowth has not been shown to last longer than 48 weeks in large clinical trials with continuous treatment with 5% minoxidil topical solution for men.
  • in clinical studies with mostly white men aged 18-49 years with moderate degrees of hair loss, 5% minoxidil topical solution for men provided more hair regrowth than 2% minoxidil topical solution
  • store at controlled room temperature of 20 to 25°C

Specifications

Men's Rogaine® Extra Strength is for men who have a general thinning of hair on the top of the scalp (vertex only, as shown in the attached pictures). Not intended for frontal baldness or a receding hairline.

Men's Rogaine® Extra Strength has been shown to regrow hair in men with hair loss in their hair vertices.
